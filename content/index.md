# CSSGB Study Notes

Study notes for the ASQ Certified Six Sigma Green Belt (CSSGB) examination.

All content is grounded in the *ASQ Certified Six Sigma Green Belt Handbook* (2nd ed.) and the *CSSC Six Sigma Green Belt Training Manual* (2018).

The exam is 110 questions (100 scored) covering six DMAIC phases:

| Phase | Questions |
|---|---|
| I — Overview: Six Sigma and the Organization | 11 |
| II — Define | 20 |
| III — Measure | 20 |
| IV — Analyze | 18 |
| V — Improve | 16 |
| VI — Control | 15 |
